How the work gets done

The Very Nice Process

01

The Brief

Every project starts with a proper conversation. Goals, audience, and priorities are captured and documented so the work always has something to come back to.

02

The Plan

Before design begins, a clear project plan is in place. Scope, timeline, and deliverables are laid out so there are no questions about what’s being built or when.

03

The Build

Design executes in weekly sprints. Work is shared at each milestone so you can be updated on progress. Feedback is collected and incorporated before the next sprint begins.

04

The Launch

Completed work is delivered in full with a handoff session covering everything needed to move forward confidently. A two-week check-in follows to make sure everything is running smoothly.
