How I Work

Four steps. No surprises.

01

Understand

A free 30-minute call to understand your goals, constraints, and what success looks like. If I'm not the right fit, I'll be upfront and point you to someone who is.

02

Scope

Platform selection, timeline, and a clear plan before any design work begins. You'll know exactly what's happening, when, and why.

03

Design

Weekly sprints with async updates. You see work early and often, so there aren't any big reveals. Every decision is tied to a user need or business goal.

04

Deliver

Polished deliverables, clean handoff, and a two-week revision window. Post-launch support is always part of the plan.
