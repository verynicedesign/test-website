About the Studio

Design that earns
its place.

I'm Joyce Eller — founder of Very Nice Design and Senior UX Designer with 11 years of crafting digital experiences for brands like Best Buy and PepsiCo.

I believe the best digital experiences start by deeply understanding the people who use them. Every project begins with listening: to your goals, your customers' needs, and the gap between them. Then I design solutions that close that gap with clarity and intention.

Clarity over complexity
Every design decision supports the user's goal — nothing is decorative noise.
Research before pixels
I prototype and validate before committing to high-fidelity — saving time and budget.
Craft at every level
From the first wireframe to the final interaction — details matter and they show.
